Campuhan

Raise a glass to the nature of Kiyosato and the wider Hokuto Area

Escape the bustle of city life in this natural idyll boasting the stunning scenery of Mt. Yatsugatake , award-winning sake and several high-quality museums.

Don't miss

  1. Yamanashi Meijo Brewery and Suntory Hakushu Distillery
  2. Kiyosato Field Ballet in the forests of Kiyosato—one of many cultural events that take place each year
  3. Fields of sunflowers in Akeno and the flowers around Heidi Village in the summer months

Quick Facts

  • Mt. Yatsugatake means the mountain with eight peaks
  • The pure waters of this region are used to make high-quality rice, award-winning sake and delicious whisky
